Hi now you find me :)

CJ Li is an interactive media artist exploring how human emotion is mirrored and translated within technological systems. Through light, image, sound, and algorithmic interaction, her work examines the relationship between artificial intelligence, social media, and self-perception. Between logic and emotion, she asks: when technology begins to feel for us, what remains real?
