Academic & Artistic References

  • Jacques Lacan, The Mirror Stage as Formative of the I Function (1949)
  • Sherry Turkle, Alone Together (2011)
  • David Wojnarowicz, Arthur Rimbaud in New York — fragmented identity through masks and performance.
  • Sophie Calle, Take Care of Yourself — emotional labor mediated by text and technology.

Theoretical Context

Echo Maze situates itself between posthuman affect theory and media phenomenology—where machines do not simply simulate emotion, but reshape how emotion is perceived. The work asks: if empathy can be programmed, does authenticity still matter?
