Process Reflection

Throughout production, the challenge lay in maintaining a balance between technical precision and emotional abstraction. Too much structure risks killing the affect; too much chaos erases meaning.

The iterative testing of projection angles, mirror alignments, and gesture sensitivity revealed that the piece itself behaves like a thinking entity—constantly adjusting to perception.

Personal Insight

Echo Maze became a mirror for myself as much as for the viewer. The work reflects my fascination and unease toward AI empathy—how something so rational can imitate warmth so convincingly.

It also revealed that “feeling understood” is perhaps more about projection than connection.
